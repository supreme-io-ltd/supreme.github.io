Our company

Supreme Import & Export (Macau) Co., Ltd. . is a one-stop solution company in packaging, headquarters situated in Hong Kong which specialize in the areas of pharmaceutical, health care and cosmetic areas including different type of closures, dosing systems, jars, applicators, plastic and glass bottles.

Supreme is synonymous with premium quality. Our fast and reliable implementation of different request relies on our commitment of optimal quality, excellence customer service, effective logistic arrangement and our dedicated staffs.

We are improving our position in the international environment for all - round packaging solutions. The fundamental benchmark for Supreme is the trust and reliability; make us a partner worth recommending.

Quality and innovation are the key factors of success in the market. With relentless commitment towards best quality, we continue to enhance our group management, marketing and sales of packaging solution and expansion of its sales platform in Hong Kong, China, and Asia Pacific regions.

Supreme has also invested significantly in its commercial infrastructure and manages its own warehousing, logistics, regulatory, quality control, and sales and marketing operation which facilitate high degree of supply chain efficiency and flexibility in providing logistic solutions.
